With airline fleets grounded, plane recyclers bet on parts boom
https://m.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/with-airline-fleets-grounded-plane-recyclers-bet-on-parts-boom-2293748
By Allison LampertMONTREAL (Reuters) - As COVID-19 grounds swaths of airline fleets, companies that profit off the dismantling and trade of aircraft parts are seeing early signs of an expected rebound in activity as carriers accelerate plane retirements. While companies that store, dismantle, and buy and sell used aircraft parts see opportunity in parked planes, a sudden increase in the supply of used parts risks depressing prices in the estimated $3 billion a year industry, despite demand from airlines seeking to lower maintenance costs, executives and analysts say.
