European shares kick of week on strong footing, vaccine progress eyed
https://m.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/european-shares-start-week-on-firm-footing-vaccine-developments-eyed-2287766
By Shreyashi Sanyal(Reuters) - European shares bounced on Monday after a Wall Street-led rout in technology stocks kept global sentiment subdued in the previous week, with investors keeping a close watch on COVID-19 vaccine developments.Australia expects to receive its first batches of a potential COVID-19 vaccine in January after a deal was struck with CSL (AX:CSL) to manufacture two vaccines, one developed by rival AstraZeneca (L:AZN) and Oxford University, and another in CSL's own labs with the University of Queensland.
