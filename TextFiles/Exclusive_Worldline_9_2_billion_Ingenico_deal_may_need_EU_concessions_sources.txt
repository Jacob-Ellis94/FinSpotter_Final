Exclusive: Worldline $9.2 billion Ingenico deal may need EU concessions - sources
https://m.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/exclusive-worldline-92-billion-ingenico-deal-may-need-eu-concessions--sources-2288046
By Foo Yun CheeBRUSSELS (Reuters) - French payments company Worldline's (PA:WLN) 7.8 billion euro ($9.23 billion) bid for  Ingenico  (PA:INGC) may require concessions to gain European Union antitrust regulator approval, people familiar with the matter said on Monday.The acquisition by Worldline, which was born out of French IT company Atos (PA:ATOS), is emblematic of a wave of mergers and acquisitions that U.S. rivals kicked off last year as they try to build up their share of digital transactions.
