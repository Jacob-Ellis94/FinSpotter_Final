Israel stocks higher at close of trade; TA 35 up 0.61%
https://m.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/israel-stocks-higher-at-close-of-trade-ta-35-up-061-2293925
Investing.com – Israel stocks were higher after the close on Sunday, as gains in the Banking, Financials and Biomed sectors led shares higher.At the close in Tel Aviv, the TA 35 gained 0.61%.The best performers of the session on the TA 35 were  Electra  Ltd (TASE:ELTR), which rose 4.11% or 5910 points to trade at 149830 at the close. Meanwhile,  Shapir Engineering Industry  (TASE:SPEN) added 3.63% or 84 points to end at 2398 and  ICL  Israel Chemicals Ltd (TASE:ICL) was up 2.92% or 35 points to 1232 in late trade.
