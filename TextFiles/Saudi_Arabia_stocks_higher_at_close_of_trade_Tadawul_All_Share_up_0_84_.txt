Saudi Arabia stocks higher at close of trade; Tadawul All Share up 0.84%
https://m.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/saudi-arabia-stocks-higher-at-close-of-trade-tadawul-all-share-up-084-2293871
Investing.com – Saudi Arabia stocks were higher after the close on Sunday, as gains in the Media & Publishing, Industrial Investment and Building & Construction sectors led shares higher.At the close in Saudi Arabia, the Tadawul All Share rose 0.84% to hit a new 6-months high.The best performers of the session on the Tadawul All Share were  Lazurde for Jewelry Co  (SE:4011), which rose 10.00% or 1.94 points to trade at 21.34 at the close. Meanwhile, Saudi Transport & Investment Co (SE:4110) added 10.00% or 3.00 points to end at 33.00 and BURUJ COOPERATIVE INSURANCE CO (SE:8270) was up 10.00% or 2.04 points to 22.44 in late trade.
